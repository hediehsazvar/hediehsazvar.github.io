About me

I am a PhD candidate in Operations Research at Northwestern University (IEMS). My research focuses on optimization and stochastic modeling for online matching platforms, motivated by healthcare staffing applications such as nurse-to-shift assignments.

I study sequential recommendation and assignment when agents arrive over time with heterogeneous preferences and uncertain commitment (no-shows). My work develops policy structure results and scalable algorithms (MIP formulations, decomposition, and heuristics) to maximize filled jobs and expected revenue under capacity constraints and overbooking.
