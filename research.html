Research

Sequential job recommendation under no-shows

I study online matching platforms where agents arrive sequentially and the platform recommends limited-capacity jobs. Agents may accept and later no-show; cancellations can create unsellable capacity. My work develops optimal policy structure and scalable optimization methods to maximize filled jobs and expected revenue.

Keywords: sequential decision-making · stochastic optimization · overbooking · MIP formulations · heuristics
