About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Operations Research in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences at Northwestern University, advised by Professor Seyed M.R. Iravani and Professor Sanjay Mehrotra. I expect to graduate in summer 2026.

My research interests are in stochastic optimization and large-scale optimization, with applications in revenue management, assortment optimization, and matching.
